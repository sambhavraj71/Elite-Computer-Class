Detailed Curriculum

  • Module 1: Computer Fundamentals - Windows OS, file management, internet basics, typing practice
  • Module 2: Adobe PageMaker - Document setup, text formatting, master pages, layout design
  • Module 3: Adobe Photoshop - Layers, selections, photo retouching, filters, effects
  • Module 4: CorelDraw - Vector graphics, logo design, text effects, patterns
  • Practical Projects - Business cards, brochures, banners, social media graphics

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need design experience before joining?

No prior design experience is required. The course starts with computer fundamentals before moving to design software. However, creative aptitude and basic computer skills will help you progress faster.

Which software versions will we learn?

We train on the latest versions of Photoshop, CorelDraw and PageMaker available in our lab. The core concepts remain the same across versions, making your skills transferable.

Will I get a certificate?

Yes, you'll receive a Diploma in Desktop Publishing upon successful completion, with online verification. While not an Adobe/Corel certification, it demonstrates your DTP proficiency to employers.

What computer specs do I need for practice at home?

We recommend at least 4GB RAM, 500GB HDD, and a dedicated graphics card for smooth performance. However, our lab computers are available for practice during institute hours.

What are the job prospects after DTP?

DTP graduates find opportunities in advertising agencies, printing presses, publishing houses, and corporate design departments. Many also pursue freelance opportunities.
