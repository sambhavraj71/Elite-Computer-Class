Comprehensive 3-Month Accounting Program

The Certificate in Financial Accounting (CFA) provides hands-on training in computerized accounting using Tally.ERP9, covering fundamental accounting principles to advanced GST compliance.

Official Tally.ERP9 curriculum included

Detailed Curriculum

  • Module 1: Accounting Fundamentals - Computer basics, bookkeeping principles, journal entries, ledger maintenance
  • Module 2: Tally.ERP9 Essentials - Software navigation, company creation, accounting vouchers, basic reports
  • Module 3: Inventory & GST - Stock management, GST implementation, tax invoices, GST returns
  • Module 4: Advanced Features - Payroll processing, balance sheet preparation, profit & loss statements, practical projects
  • Real-World Projects - Complete accounting cycles for sample businesses

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need accounting knowledge before joining?

No prior accounting knowledge is required. The course starts with basic accounting principles before moving to Tally.ERP9. However, commerce background students may grasp concepts faster.

Is this course suitable for business owners?

Absolutely! Many small business owners take this course to manage their own accounts and GST filing. The practical approach is perfect for business applications.

What version of Tally will we learn?

We train on Tally.ERP9 with the latest GST updates. The skills are transferable to newer versions of Tally as the core functionality remains similar.

Will I get a certificate?

Yes, you'll receive a Certificate in Financial Accounting with online verification. While not a Tally certification, it demonstrates your accounting software proficiency to employers.

What are the job prospects after CFA?

CFA graduates find opportunities as Tally operators, junior accountants, billing executives in various industries. GST knowledge is particularly valued by employers.
