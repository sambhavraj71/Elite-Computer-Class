Certificate in Financial Accounting Program

The Certificate in Financial Accounting (CFA) is designed for students who want to build a career in accounting and finance. This comprehensive program covers accounting fundamentals, Tally ERP 9, GST compliance, and inventory management - perfect for aspiring accountants and bookkeepers.

MS Windows
Tally ERP 9
GST Software
Internet Banking

Module 1: Computer Operating Fundamentals

  • MS-Windows 7/8/10: Windows interface, file management, and system operations
  • Internet: Online banking, email, and web browsing for accounting

Module 2: Accounting Introduction

  • Book Keeping: Fundamental principles of maintaining financial records
  • Account & Journal: Understanding accounts and journal entry methods
  • Ledger: Creating and maintaining ledger accounts
  • Trial Balance: Preparing and balancing trial balance
  • Financial Statements: Understanding financial statement preparation
  • Trading Account: Preparing trading accounts for businesses
  • Profit and Loss Account: Creating P&L statements
  • Balance Sheet (Position Statement): Understanding balance sheet preparation

Module 3: Account Only

  • Introduction to Tally ERP 9: Getting started with Tally software
  • Create and Select Company: Setting up company profiles in Tally
  • Accounts Information and Account Group: Managing account groups
  • Charts of Accounts: Creating comprehensive account charts
  • Create a Ledger and Voucher Entry Screen: Ledger creation and voucher management
  • Voucher Creation of Group and Ledger: Advanced voucher operations
  • Purchase and Sales Voucher: Managing purchase and sales transactions
  • Journal Voucher & Memo Voucher: Creating journal and memo entries
  • Report: Generating various accounting reports

Module 4: Account with Inventory

  • Invoice Entry: Creating and managing invoices
  • Introduction Account with Inventory: Inventory accounting basics
  • Inventory Vouchers: Managing inventory transactions
  • Display Inventory Reports & Statements: Generating inventory reports
  • Cost Categories & Cost Centres: Managing cost accounting
  • Discount: Handling discount calculations
  • Payroll: Basic payroll management
  • Manufacturing: Manufacturing account management
  • TDS: Tax Deducted at Source calculations
  • Goods and Services Tax (GST): GST compliance and filing
  • Project Work: Comprehensive practical projects

Career Opportunities

CFA graduates can pursue positions in:

  • Accountant in various organizations
  • Bookkeeper in small and medium businesses
  • Accounts Assistant in corporate offices
  • Tally Operator in accounting firms
  • Finance Executive in companies
  • Tax Assistant for GST compliance

Learning Outcomes

After completing this course, you will be able to:

  • Understand fundamental accounting principles and practices
  • Operate Tally ERP 9 for complete accounting management
  • Prepare financial statements and reports
  • Handle GST compliance and tax calculations
  • Manage inventory and cost accounting
  • Work confidently in accounting and finance roles

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our CFA certificate program

Do I need prior accounting experience for CFA?

No prior accounting experience is required. CFA is designed for beginners. We start with basic accounting principles and gradually build your expertise.

Which software will I learn in the CFA course?

You will learn Tally ERP 9, MS Windows, and internet applications. Tally ERP 9 is the most important accounting software used by businesses across India.

Can I get a job after completing CFA?

Yes, CFA provides you with accounting skills needed for positions like accountant, bookkeeper, Tally operator, and accounts assistant roles.

What kind of practical work will I do during the course?

You'll practice creating company accounts in Tally, preparing financial statements, managing inventory, processing GST returns, and handling real accounting scenarios.

Is there job placement assistance after CFA?

Yes, we provide job placement assistance and help you connect with accounting firms, businesses, and organizations looking for qualified accounting professionals.
