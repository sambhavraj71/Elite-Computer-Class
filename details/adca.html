Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between DCA and ADCA?

ADCA is an advanced version that includes DCA (basic computer applications) plus CFA (computerized financial accounting) and DTP (desktop publishing). It provides three certifications in one comprehensive program.

Do I need any prior computer knowledge?

No prior knowledge is required. The course starts from basic computer operations and gradually builds up to advanced applications. However, basic familiarity with computers is helpful.

Is Tally included in the course fee?

Yes, Tally.ERP 9 training with GST implementation is included in the standard curriculum at no extra cost. We provide practice license for educational use during the course.

Can I get a job after completing ADCA?

Yes, ADCA graduates have excellent job prospects in various fields including accounting firms, offices, publishing houses, and more. We provide placement assistance to our students.

What's the class schedule like?

We offer flexible batches - morning (9am-12pm), afternoon (2pm-5pm), and evening (6pm-9pm). You can attend 3 hours per day, 5 days a week. Weekend batches are also available.
