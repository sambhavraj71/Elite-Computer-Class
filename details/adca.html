Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our ADCA program

What's the difference between DCA and ADCA?

ADCA is an advanced version that includes DCA (basic computer applications) plus CFA (computerized financial accounting) and DTP (desktop publishing). It provides three certifications in one comprehensive program, making you more versatile and employable.

Do I need any prior computer knowledge?

No prior knowledge is required. The course starts from basic computer operations and gradually builds up to advanced applications. However, basic familiarity with computers is helpful for faster learning.

Is Tally training included in the course fee?

Yes, Tally.ERP 9 training with GST implementation is included in the standard curriculum at no extra cost. We provide practice licenses for educational use during the course.

What are the job prospects after ADCA?

ADCA graduates have excellent job prospects in various fields including accounting firms, corporate offices, publishing houses, government departments, and more. We also provide placement assistance to our students.

What's the class schedule and duration?

We offer flexible batches - morning (9am-12pm), afternoon (2pm-5pm), and evening (6pm-9pm). Classes are 3 hours per day, 5 days a week. Weekend batches are also available for working professionals.
