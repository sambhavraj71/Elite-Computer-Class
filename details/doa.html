Detailed Curriculum

  • Module 1: Operating System & Computer Fundamentals - Windows OS, file management, internet basics, typing practice
  • Module 2: Office Package - MS Word (document processing), Excel (spreadsheets), PowerPoint (presentations)
  • Module 3: Database Management - MS Access, MySQL for data organization and retrieval
  • Module 4: Information Technology - HTML basics, multimedia applications, internet technologies
  • Practical Skills - Networking fundamentals, hardware maintenance, office automation workflows

Frequently Asked Questions

What computer skills do I need before joining?

Basic computer literacy is helpful but not required. The course starts with computer fundamentals before moving to office applications. We'll teach you everything from turning on the computer to advanced automation techniques.

Which versions of office software will we learn?

We train on the latest versions of Microsoft Office available in our lab, but the skills are transferable to any version. We also cover open-source alternatives like OpenOffice for students who may not have access to commercial software.

Is there any age limit for this course?

No, there's no age limit. The course is suitable for school graduates, college students, working professionals, and even retirees looking to gain computer skills. Our youngest student was 16 and our oldest was 68!

What kind of jobs can I get after completing this?

Graduates find positions as office assistants, data entry operators, computer operators, administrative assistants, and more in various industries including banking, education, healthcare, and government sectors.

Can I take this course while working full-time?

Absolutely! We offer flexible batch timings including evening classes to accommodate working professionals. The 9-month duration allows for manageable weekly commitments.
