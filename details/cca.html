Course Overview

The Certificate in Computer Application (CCA) is designed to provide fundamental knowledge of computer operations and applications. This 3-month intensive program covers all essential aspects of computer usage that are required in today's digital world.

What You'll Learn

  • Operating System & Computer Fundamentals - Understand how computers work
  • Comprehensive introduction to MS-Office 2010 applications
  • Working with MS-DOS and modern Windows environments (7/8/10)
  • Mastering Microsoft Word for document creation and formatting
  • Excel proficiency including formulas, charts, and data analysis
  • Creating powerful presentations with PowerPoint
  • Practical projects to apply your learning in real scenarios

Who Should Attend?

This course is perfect for beginners, students, job seekers, and professionals who want to improve their computer skills. No prior computer knowledge is required.

Career Opportunities

After completing this course, you'll be prepared for various entry-level positions like Data Entry Operator, Office Assistant, Computer Operator, and more in both government and private sectors.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the eligibility criteria for this course?

There are no strict eligibility criteria for the CCA course. Basic understanding of English is helpful but not mandatory. The course is designed for absolute beginners.

What are the class timings?

We offer flexible batches - morning (10 AM - 12 PM), afternoon (2 PM - 4 PM), and evening (6 PM - 8 PM). Weekend batches are also available.

Is there any exam at the end of the course?

Yes, there will be a practical and theoretical examination at the end of the course. You'll also be evaluated based on your assignments and projects.

Do you provide placement assistance?

While we don't guarantee placements, we do provide placement assistance including resume preparation guidance and information about job openings.

Can I pay the fee in installments?

Yes, the fee can be paid in installments as mentioned. ₹1150 admission fee at the time of registration and two installments of ₹500 each during the course.
